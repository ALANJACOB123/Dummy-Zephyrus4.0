Zephyrus is a technical festival hosted by the department of computer science every year. It involves a lot of events in which students can participate and show their expertise.

The events bring out the best in ever student and help them to grow in their field of interest. It also helps the students who organise the fest to manage a event and gain a lot of event management qualities.
